About

I am an MSc student and Research Lead at the ATLAS Lab (AI Trust, Learning, Architecture, and Security) at the Technion – Israel Institute of Technology. My research focuses on understanding and exposing vulnerabilities in safety-aligned language models, including adversarial jailbreaks, silenced biases, and refusal dynamics.

Beyond academic research, I actively engage with the security community — supervising work presented at BlackHat / SecTor, and speaking at venues including Google DevFest, Zenity, and Team8 on the intersection of AI and cybersecurity.
